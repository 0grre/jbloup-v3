About me

A french web developer for nearly 5 years, my professional path has been quite unique. After exploring several very different jobs, ranging from postal worker to train driver and paramedic, I finally retrained in 2019, thereby discovering my true professional calling: computer development.

What started as a career has become a hobby. I often spend my free time coding. I am particularly drawn to the world of open source for its spirit of sharing and innovation.

Outside of development, my interests are varied: I am an avid amateur astronomer and space enthusiast, a committed aquarist, and recently a rugby player. This diversity of interests feeds my creativity and my approach to development.
